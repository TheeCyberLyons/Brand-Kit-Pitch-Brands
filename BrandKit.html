About Just Snazz

Jasmine Lyons aka Just Snazz is a Detroit-born hip hop artist, TikTok creator, and cultural curator who creates content at the intersection of music, nostalgia, and community. Known for her skits, storytelling, playlists, and music-first content, she's built an authentic, dedicated audience of 10.8K+ on TikTok—mostly music lovers aged 27–45 who appreciate creativity, humor, and the real.


Her platform blends original music, playlist discovery, freestyles, commentary, and niche genre love (from ghetto tech to alt-R&B). As a Black lesbian artist, her content brings often underrepresented voices to the forefront in a way that resonates far beyond her hometown.
